D365 CE Context Exporter

This plugin connects to your Dataverse environment and exports structured context files ready to be shared with an AI assistant. It queries your data, applies a template, and produces a concise .context.md document that gives the AI the right background to answer questions about your specific setup.

  1. Connect — connect XrmToolBox to your Dataverse environment.
  2. Pick a base directory — click the folder picker and choose (or create) an empty folder. On first use the plugin will offer to deploy the reference configuration there.
  3. Run first-time setup — accept the prompt to deploy sample specs, FetchXML queries, and Scriban templates to the folder.
  4. Select a spec — choose one of the available specs from the dropdown (several samples are provided out of the box).
  5. Click Run — the plugin executes the queries, renders the template, and saves the output to output\<SpecName>.context.md.
  6. Share with your AI assistant — attach or paste the .context.md file into your AI conversation as grounding context.

The config folder holds the specs, FetchXML queries, and Scriban templates that drive the export. These files are meant to be modified and extended to fit your needs — or even adapted to carry custom instructions for a specific AI agent.
